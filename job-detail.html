Data Engineer

San Jose, California, United States of America Full Time $123 - $456

Job description

A Data Engineer designs, builds, and maintains systems that collect, store, and process large amounts of data. They create data pipelines, ensure data quality, and prepare data for analysis. Their work supports data scientists and analysts by providing clean, organized, and accessible data for business insights and decision-making.

Responsibility

Here are the main responsibilities of a Data Engineer:

  • Design and build reliable data pipelines.
  • Develop and manage databases and large-scale data systems.
  • Clean, organize, and transform raw data into usable formats.
  • Ensure data quality, security, and privacy.
  • Optimize data storage and retrieval for efficiency.
